About NotiZAR

Protecting Tshwane communities through innovative technology and community collaboration since 2023

Our Mission

NotiZAR was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to create safer communities through technology-enabled collaboration. We believe that when communities are connected, informed, and empowered, they become their own best defense against crime and emergencies.

Our mission is to bridge the critical gap between traditional emergency response systems and the real-time needs of modern communities. We provide the tools and platform for neighbors to protect neighbors, creating a network of safety that extends beyond individual households to entire neighborhoods.

Our Story

NotiZAR was born at the Tshwane Vision Hackathon (TVH) 2025 held at Tshwane University of Technology (TUT), where a team of passionate students and community activists came together to address the growing safety concerns in Tshwane communities.

Inspired by the frequent cable theft incidents and security challenges facing local neighborhoods, our founders developed the initial prototype that would evolve into the comprehensive community protection platform we have today. The hackathon provided the perfect environment to combine technical innovation with real community needs.

What started as a 48-hour hackathon project has grown into a fully-fledged community safety solution serving thousands of households across Tshwane. Our journey from hackathon prototype to community platform demonstrates the power of collaborative innovation in solving real-world problems.

Our Milestones

  • 2025: Born at TVH Hackathon at TUT - Initial prototype development
  • 2025: First pilot launch in Hatfield and surrounding areas
  • 2025: Expanded to 15+ communities across Tshwane
  • 2025: Reached 5,000+ protected homes within first 6 months
  • 2025: Integrated real-time mapping and AI threat detection
