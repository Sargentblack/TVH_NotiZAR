About NotiZAR

Born at TVH Hackathon 2025 - Protecting Tshwane communities through innovation

Our Mission

NotiZAR was created to bridge the gap between communities and emergency response systems in Tshwane. We believe that timely information and coordinated action can save lives and protect property during critical situations.

Our Story

Born at the Tshwane Vision Hackathon 2025 at TUT, NotiZAR started as a student project and has evolved into a comprehensive protection platform serving thousands of households across Pretoria and surrounding areas.

Our Team

We're a dedicated team of students, developers, and community organizers committed to making Tshwane safer for everyone.
